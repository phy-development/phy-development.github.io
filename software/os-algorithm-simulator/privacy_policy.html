Privacy Policy

Personal Data

OS Algorithm Simulator does not collect any personal data of the users.

Other Data

Learning scenarios are stored in your own device, in the public directory of your choice. You can access, modify and erase that data by using either OS Algorithm Simulator or a file manager. Data will stay in the device even after uninstalling the application, until it is manually removed by the user.
