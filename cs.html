Rafael López García

Academic background in Computer Science

  • B.S. Degree in Computing Science from the University of A Coruña (UDC) in October 2006. The final project "Design and implementation of an interactive service for IPTV", mentee by the late Associate Professor Ph.D. Víctor M. Gulías Fernández, received a qualification of "excellent".
  • Internship at Cinfo from October 2006 to March 2007.
  • Master's of Advanced Studies (DEA) from the former Department of Information and Communication Technologies (TIC) of the University of A Coruña in July 2009. The Master's Thesis, mentee by Associate Professor Ph.D. Fernando Bellas Permuy, received a qualification of "excellent".
  • B.S. Degree in System Engineering from the University of A Coruña (UDC) in March 2010. The final project "Framework for Teaching and Research in Information Retrieval", mentee by Associate Professor Ph.D. Carlos Alberto Pan Bermúdez, received a qualification of "excellent". Described here.
  • Ph.D. candidate at Department of Social Informatics, Kyoto University (in Japanese 京都大学), mentee by Professor Ph.D. Katsumi Tanaka (in Japanese 田中克己教授) and, after his retirement, by Professor Ph.D. Masatoshi Yoshikawa (in Japanese 吉川正俊教授).
  • Participation in several research projects.
  • Currently studying a Master's Degree in Cibersecurity, jointly held by University of A Coruña and University of Vigo.

  • Attended several courses about computer science at:

    • Centro Autónomo de Formación e Innovación (CAFI) - Consellería de Cultura, Educación e Universidade - Xunta de Galicia.
    • Aula de Formación Informática (AFI) - University of A Coruña.
