From MakeMusic, Inc.

MakeMusic, Inc. is a world leader in music technology. Their products include Finale, the world’s best-selling music notation software; Garritan, the leading provider of quality virtual software instruments; MusicXML, the standard open format for exchanging digital sheet music; and SmartMusic, interactive software featuring the world’s largest accompaniment library.

On Septeber 9th, 2016, Michael Good from MakeMusic, Inc. in the news about MusicXML compliant apps, commented on Kunkunshi Editor: "This may be my favorite as it is the most unusual. Kunkunshi Editor is an Android app for editing Okinawan music in Kunkunshi (工工四) format. This is a vertical, tabular format of music notation which is a variation of Chinese music notation systems".

See the original post here.

Indeed, Kunkunshi Editor has been listed as one of the 225 applications and libraries that are compliant with MusicXML (see list here).
