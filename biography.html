Rafael López García

Biography

Rafael López García was born in Ferrol, A Coruña (Spain) in 1982.

He obtained both his B.S. Degree in Computing Science and his Master's of Advanced Studies in Information and Communication Technologies from the University of A Coruña, where he started a Ph.D too. In those same years, he got his teacher license from the same university. He also studied advanced English and started learning Japanese.

When he had the chance, he moved to Japan as he became a Ph.D. candidate at Kyoto University (in Japanese, 京都大学), where he also continued learning Japanese. He stayed in Japan from 2012 to 2017, where he did a part-time job as an English and Spanish teacher and he learned traditional music instruments such as shamisen and sanshin too. Moreover, it was also in those years when he started developing Android applications, making his debut with Kunkunshi Editor.

In 2017, he was appointed as a teacher of System Administration at vocational school in Galicia, Spain. His first position was at CIFP Rodolfo Ucha Piñeiro, where he won a teaching innovation price in 2019. At the same time, he started learning French and continued studying the highest levels of advanced English.

Since 2019, he has been working as ICT Consultant at Asesoría de Bibliotecas Escolares - Consellería de Cultura, Educación e Universidade - Xunta de Galicia. In addition, he studied basic Portuguese.

In 2020 he started a Master's Degree in Cibersecurity, jointly held by University of A Coruña and University of Vigo.

His primary fields of interest in IT are Web Technologies, Mobile Applications, Cybersecurity, Information Retrieval. He is also very interested in Education.
