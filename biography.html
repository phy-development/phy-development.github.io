Rafael López García

Biography

Rafael López García was born in Ferrol, A Coruña (Spain) in 1982.

He obtained both his B.S. Degree in Computing Science and his Master's of Advanced Studies in Information and Communication Technologies from the University of A Coruña, where he started a Ph.D too. In those same years, he got his teacher license from the same university. He also studied advanced English and started learning Japanese at Escola Oficial de Idiomas de A Coruña.

When he had the chance, he moved to Japan as he became a Ph.D. candidate at Kyoto University (in Japanese, 京都大学), where he also continued learning Japanese. He stayed in Jpaan from 2012 to 2017, where he did a part-time job as an English and Spanish teacher too.

In 2017, he was appointed as a teacher of System Administration at vocational school in Galicia, Spain. His first position was at CIFP Rodolfo Ucha Piñeiro, where he won a teaching innovation price FP Innova. At the same time, he started learning French.

Since 2019, he has been working as ICT Consultant at Asesoría de Bibliotecas Escolares - Consellería de Cultura, Educación e Universidade - Xunta de Galicia. In addition, he studied basci Portuguese.

Since 2020, he is also studying a Master's Degree in Cibersecurity, jointly held by University of A Coruña and University of Vigo.

His primary fields of interest in IT are Web Technologies, Mobile Applications, Cybersecurity, Information Retrieval. He is also very interested in Education.
