Rafael López García

Biography

Rafael López García was born in Ferrol, A Coruña (Spain) on the 8th of December of 1982.

He obtained both his B.S. Degree in Computing Science and his Master's Degree in Information and Communication Technologies from the University of A Coruña and was a Ph.D. candidate at Kyoto University (in Japanese, 京都大学) from 2012 to 2017, when he was appointed as a teacher of System Administration at technical school in Galicia, Spain. Since 2019, he has been working as ICT Consultant at Asesoría de Bibliotecas Escolares - Consellería de Educación e Ordenación Universitaria - Xunta de Galicia. At the same time, he is studying a Master's Degree in Cibersecurity at University of A Coruña.

His primary fields of interest in IT are Web Technologies, Mobile Applications, Cybersecurity, Information Retrieval and Education.
